CelcomDigi Event

On 29 April 2025, I attended the CelcomDigi UniConnect which was established at UTM, a collaborative industry-academia program aimed at bridging the gap between classroom knowledge and real-world industry practices. Organized by CelcomDigi in partnership with UTM, the event featured insightful talks, career-building workshops, and networking sessions with professionals from the telecommunications and tech industry. The event provided a unique opportunity for students to gain firsthand insights into current trends in digital innovation, 5G applications, and future career pathways in Malaysia's fast-evolving tech landscape.

  • Event Takeaways
    The Future is Digital-First – One of the key insights was how digital transformation is reshaping all industries. The speakers emphasized the importance of developing digital skills, such as cloud computing, data analytics, and mobile development, to stay competitive in the evolving job market.
  • Soft Skills Matter – Beyond technical knowledge, the importance of communication, adaptability, and a growth mindset was repeatedly highlighted. Industry leaders shared real examples of how these traits play a crucial role in both hiring decisions and long-term career development.
